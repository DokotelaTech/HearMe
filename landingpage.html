Breaking down barriers to mental health support through community, AI, and professional care


Our mission


HearMe is a mental health support platform designed to provide accessible, stigma-free assistance through a unique blend of community connection, AI-powered support, and professional social worker integration. We believe everyone deserves a safe space to express their feelings, receive empathetic support, and access professional help when needed—without judgment, without barriers, and without breaking the bank.
